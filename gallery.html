Meditation

By learning to calm your body and mind, your physical and emotional stress can melt away. This leaves you feeling better, refreshed, and ready to face the challenges of your day with a healthy attitude.

Amazing Views

Dzogchen Beara is a Meditation Retreat centre situated on the Beara Peninsula in southwest Ireland. It sits high on cliffs overlooking the Atlantic Ocean with breathtaking views of the sea and sky.

Cafe and Shop

Enjoy tasty food, good company and a nice shopping experience We,re open to the public seven days a week from 10am-5pm so do drop in if you are in the area.

Shrine Room

This installation allows visitors to experience a sacred space that is found in a Tibetan household. The religious objects in the shrine would be displayed and used in ritual and devotional practices.
